FAQ

How does it work?
The app has 3 parts:
  • For the client to find an uberstylist nearby. To keep a record of their different hairstyles and be able to share their new look. If out of town, allow their own uberstylist to release the technical information to get that colour just right.
  • For the stylist to manage their appointments and client-cards.
  • For the salon to manage a group of stylists (who manage themselves :-)
What will it cost?
  • For the client absolutely free.
  • For the stylist $9.99 per year.
  • For the salon $99 per year.
